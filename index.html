Our Features

Idea Suggestion

TRACKSY elevates your content production by generating fresh video ideas with the help of AI when high-performing videos on your YouTube channel are detected.

Comparison

TRACKSY enables you to compare videos, channels and different video formats, easily identifying differences. You can assign your videos to those formats within our app.

Trend Detection

TRACKSY automatically monitors the performance of videos on your YouTube channel and notifies you of newly detected trends. No more tedious manual analysis!

Comment Sentiment

TRACKSY performs an AI-powered sentiment analysis on the comments of your videos to give you more insights on how your video is received by your audience.

About Us

TRACKSY is designed as a creative app that enhances the productivity of your YouTube Channel. Our platform excels in detecting trending videos and suggesting innovative video ideas. Specifically designed for YouTube content creators, TRACKSY provides valuable insights into performance metrics and audience engagement.

TRACKSY integrates generative AI into its features, incorporating features like video idea suggestions and comment sentiment analysis. This provides users with actionable insights derived from YouTube data. To achieve this, our server architecture is built to seamlessly incorporate OpenAI services such as ChatGPT 3.5 and DALL·E 3.
