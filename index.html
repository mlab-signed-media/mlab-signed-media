Amazing Features To Use
TRACKSY

Comparison

For the video and channel comparisons, the user can decide on the category they want to compare by selecting either the video category, channel category, or format-based category.

Comment Sentiment

For each uploaded video on your YouTube channel, comment sentiment analysis can be performed, providing percentages and top comments for each category.

Trend Detection

When a new video is uploaded, the recurring background job on the backend recognizes this and adds the video to the watchlist. The metrics for that video are then updated every hour. All the videos on the watchlist are subsequently analyzed regularly to check whether a trend has been detected.

Idea Suggestion

When a new trend emerges on the timeline, TRACKSY generates new video ideas. The ChatGPT prompt for video suggestions is then enriched with trend analysis details, including the video title, keywords, and whether it's associated with a positive or negative trend.

About Us

TRACKSY is designed to be a comprehensive analytics platform leveraging the YouTube API for in-depth video and channel analysis as well as the detection of trending videos and the suggestion of more video ideas. The app addresses the need for a more sophisticated tool for Signed Media's Arte Tracks YouTube channel, enhancing the decision-making process for content creation as well as overviewing audience engagement, and overall channel and video metrics.

TRACKSY incorporates machine learning in its features including trend analysis and comment sentiment analysis, to empower users with actionable insights derived from YouTube data and a generative model
